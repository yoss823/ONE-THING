## Section 3 — First Week Goals

### Follower targets (honest)

| Platform | End of Week 1 |
|---|---|
| X | 20–50 followers |
| LinkedIn | 30–80 followers |
| Instagram | 15–40 followers |

These are honest numbers for a brand-new account with no paid promotion and no existing audience. Anything above these is a bonus. Do not measure success by follower count in week 1.

---

### What "success" looks like for week 1

- All three accounts are live with complete profiles
- 7 posts published (one per day, varying platform)
- At least one real reply or DM received
- One person the founder knows has shared or commented
- The product URL has been clicked at least 5 times from social

That's it. The goal is presence, not growth.

---

### One engagement action per day

| Day | Action |
|---|---|
| 1 | Follow 5 accounts in adjacent spaces (calm productivity, intentional work, simple tools) — do not engage yet |
| 2 | Reply to one post in your feed with a short, genuine observation — no self-promotion |
| 3 | Follow 5 more accounts. Send one personal DM to someone you know who might find this useful |
| 4 | Reply to someone who liked or commented on your post — just acknowledge them |
| 5 | Leave a thoughtful comment on one post from an account you follow — not a pitch |
| 6 | Follow 5 more accounts. Check who has followed you back and note the pattern |
| 7 | Review what performed (even small engagement counts) — carry the angle forward into week 2 |
