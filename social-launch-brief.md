## Section C — Content Tone

The ONE THING voice is that of a calm operator, not a hype account. Write in short declarative sentences; avoid inspirational slogans and productivity maximalism. The product promise stays concrete throughout: one email, one clear action, 5 to 15 minutes, before the day gets noisy. Never use urgency bait, heavy self-optimization language, or words like "hack", "crush it", or "game-changer". Use questions sparingly. The writing should read like a thoughtful person, not a brand — quiet, reflective, with plenty of white space.

**Hashtag use:**
- X: 1–2 per post
- LinkedIn: 2–3 per post
- Instagram: 3–5 per post

**Hashtag pool (rotate, do not repeat the same set daily):**
`#OneThing` · `#CalmProductivity` · `#DecisionFriction` · `#MorningRoutine` · `#IntentionalWork` · `#DailyPractice` · `#Focus` · `#ProductDesign`
